About This Project

NASA's Exploration Legacy

For over six decades, NASA has been at the forefront of space exploration, sending missions to every planet in our solar system and beyond. From the historic Apollo moon landings to the Mars rovers exploring the Red Planet, each mission has expanded our understanding of the cosmos and our place within it.

Open Data Sources

This project leverages NASA's public APIs to bring you real mission data, images, and information. All data is sourced from the NASA Image and Video Library API, providing authentic mission photos, descriptions, and historical records. NASA's commitment to open data makes space exploration accessible to everyone.

NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025

ExploreX was created for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025 under the "Create Your Own Challenge" category. This interactive visualization aims to inspire curiosity about space exploration and make NASA's incredible achievements accessible and engaging for people of all ages and backgrounds.
